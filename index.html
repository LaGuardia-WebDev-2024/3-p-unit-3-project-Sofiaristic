Cinnamon sugar apple cake

Recipe #1: Cinnamon Sugar Apple Cake

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook time: 40 mins

Serves: 15

Ingredients Quantity
Apple cider 1 1/2 cups
Melted cocunut oil 1/3 cup
Vanilla extract 1 Tablespoon
Eggs 1 egg
honey or maple syrup 1/2 cup
Apple butter 1/3 cup
all purpose flour 2 cups
baking soda 1 teaspoon
kosher salt 1/2 teaspoon
apple/pumpkin spice 2-3 teaspoons
honey crisp apples 2-3 apples (sliced)
cold salted buter,grated 6 tablespoons
granulated sugar 1/2 cup
cinnamon 2 teaspoons

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350° F. Line a 9×13 inch baking dish with parchment paper

Step 2: Bring the apple cider to a boil over high heat. Reduce the heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes, until reduced to 1/2 cup. Remove from the heat. Let cool.

Step 3: stir together the boiled cider, coconut oil, vanilla, egg, maple syrup, and apple butter, until combined. Add the flour, baking soda, apple/pumpkin pie spice, and salt. Mix until just combined. Fold in the apples. The batter will be thick.

Step 4:Spread the batter evenly among the prepared dish.

Step 5: In a bowl, mix the sugar, cinnamon, and grated butter until a clumpy sugar forms. Sprinkle the sugar over the surface of the cake. Bake for 30-40 minutes, until just set
